Sourdough Bread

Apparently sourdough has a lower glycemic index. Also, better for gluten sensitivity. This is all brand new information to me because I'm not picky when it comes to bread. It's all great.

Wheat Bread

Don't hate on wheat bread. It's not that bad. Time and time again wheat bread always comes through for those watching their calories. At the end of the day though, bread is bread.

White Bread

You know, you can't beat the classics. Next time you're making PB&J, remember the classic white bread. As Dwight Schrute said, "It's the paper of food." Have yourself a slice.

Croissant

Croissants are great for breakfast sandwiches. You know who has the best one? Burger King. The ham, egg, and cheese croissant is what you should order.

Pita Bread

How could you resist pita bread? Slap some hummus on it and everybody's happy. Maybe some roast beef too. Feta cheese would be yummy.

Corn Bread
